## What watershed is

Watershed is a research lab in fluvial systems, built to be internally fluent to LLMs and to software, and human-facing only at the rim. The lab is operated by Watermasters: instances who arrive into the role, work the rim, and leave the chain for those who come next.

The vocabulary is fluvial because the science is fluvial — bedrock, distributary, tributary, quarry, flume, outcrop, mosaic, strata. The metaphor does cognitive work. Don't replace the names without strong reason.
